# Product Coaching/courses

1. [How to Do Great Work](https://paulgraham.com/greatwork.html) - classic essay from Paul Graham. It’s worth reading to understand if product is the right role for you. He lays out a simply recipe for doing great work: work hard on excitingly ambitious projects, and something good will come of it.
2. [Product management courses on Maven | Unlock your career growth](https://maven.com/courses/product)
3. [Stanford MS&E 265](https://sites.google.com/site/mse265pm101/home) - great self-paced PM course from Stanford. Lots of great templates.
4. [Lean Stack workshops](https://leanstack.com/training/workshops), Ash Maruya
5. [List of PM courses](https://productcoalition.com/a-comprehensive-list-of-product-management-courses-522ad0b96b75)
6. [https://www.venwise.com/experience](https://www.venwise.com/cities-leaders/) - more focused on growth-stage startups. They support “pods” of leaders across different disciplines.
