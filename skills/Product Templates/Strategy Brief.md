Goal: Align cross-org and cross-functional teams on the strategy that will drive key initiatives over the next 18-24 months. 

**What are the problems you are solving?**

- [P0] **Problem:** Outline the problems in a few bullet points

- [P0] Key initiatives - outline the solution in a few bullet points
