## 2. Poised communicator
Enthusiastic but not overbearing. Not defensive when ideas are challenged, respectful of other views

Accepts [Brandolini's law](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Brandolini%27s_law): The amount of energy needed to refute bullshit is an order of magnitude bigger than that needed to produce it.

Accept [Miller's law](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Miller%27s_law): “that in order to understand what someone is telling you, it is necessary for you to assume the person is being truthful, then imagine what could be true about it.”

Accept [Sayre's law](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sayre%27s_law#:~:text=Sayre's%20law%20states%2C%20in%20a,(1905%E2%80%931972)%2C%20U.S.): “In any dispute the intensity of feeling is inversely proportional to the value of the issues at stake.”. E.g. Academic politics is the most vicious and bitter form of politics, because the stakes are so low.
